Speech Evaluation

Thanks for your time and welcome to the subjective evaluation of our Emotional TTS System!

First, you will need to do a self-evaluation which includes 5 questions. Then you will hear some audios followed by 5 questions about them. Please answer all questions, thanks!

After you click ‘Submit’, all your results will be recorded, and a CSV file will be downloaded. Please send the CSV file to zijiang.yang@informatik.uni-augsburg.de . Thanks for your help!

A short definition of emotions included:

  • Please select ‘Neutral’ if you think the speaker expresses nothing in particular.
  • Please select ‘Anger’ if you think the speaker is mad or furious about something.
  • Please select ‘Amused’ if you think the speaker finds something funny or interesting.
  • Please select ‘Disgust’ if you think the speaker feels nauseating.
  • Please select ‘Sleepy’ if you think the speaker is tired or exhausted.

Self-evaluation

I am an empathic person, e.g. I get sad or angry during dramatic scenes in movies.
I can easily hear and identify how a person feels in a phone conversation. I don’t need to see them to recognize their emotions.
I am musically trained, e.g. I can play a musical instrument.
High audio quality in media is important to me, e.g. I own high-end speakers.
I am good at predicting other people’s behavior , e.g. I know how my actions will make others feel.
